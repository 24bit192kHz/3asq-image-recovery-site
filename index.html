About the extension

The extension restores 3asq manga chapter images that fail to load by using exact, previously verified Wayback Machine copies. It operates only on supported 3asq domains.

The extension is independent and is not affiliated with or endorsed by the Internet Archive.

Supported websites

  • 3asq.org and its subdomains
  • 3asq.pro and its subdomains
  • 3asq.online and its subdomains
